A simple picker for Ghostty custom shaders.

In order to use this application, you must have the following in your Ghostty 
config:

    config-file = ?auto/shader.ghostty

A collection of shaders are in-built from the https://github.com/0xhckr/ghostty-shaders
repository. You can opt to not use these in-built shaders with the --include-inbuilt-shaders=false

Flags:
%s
Examples:
* Auto reload, and include shaders from directory A, and B, and inbuilt.
    ghostty-shader-picker --shader-dir A --shader-dir B --apply 
* Include shaders from directory C, and include shader files B and C.
    ghostty-shader-picker --shader-dir C --shader-file B --shader-file C
